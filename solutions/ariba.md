# SAP Ariba — Solution Knowledge Base

## Solution Overview
SAP Ariba is the world's largest B2B trading network and procurement platform, connecting over 6 million businesses. It covers the complete source-to-pay process: strategic sourcing, contract management, supplier management, procurement, and invoicing — all on a cloud network that gives buyers real-time visibility into their supply base and spend.

## Key Capabilities

### Strategic Sourcing
- RFx (RFI, RFP, RFQ) management and e-auctions
- Spend analysis and category management
- Savings tracking and project management
- AI-powered sourcing recommendations based on market data
- Integration with supplier risk scores

### Contract Management
- Contract authoring with clause library and compliance tracking
- Obligation management and renewal alerts
- AI contract analysis and deviation detection
- Linkage from contract to sourcing event to purchase order

### Supplier Management (SAP Business Network / Ariba SLP)
- Supplier onboarding, qualification, and performance management
- Supplier risk monitoring (financial stability, ESG, geopolitical)
- Preferred supplier lists and diversity tracking
- Supplier collaboration portal

### Procurement (Guided Buying / P2P)
- Consumer-like shopping experience with catalog management
- Policy compliance built into the buying experience
- Spot buy for non-catalog purchases
- Touchless PO processing for approved catalogs
- Punch-out catalog integration with major suppliers

### Invoice Management
- Supplier invoice submission via SAP Business Network
- AI-based invoice capture and 3-way match
- Exception management and dispute resolution
- Early payment discount capture and supply chain financing

### SAP Business Network
- World's largest B2B network — 6M+ connected businesses
- Direct materials collaboration (orders, ASNs, invoices)
- Logistics collaboration (freight orders, carrier integration)
- Supply chain visibility and event management

## Business Value Propositions
- **Spend under management**: Typically 60–90% of tail spend is unmanaged — Ariba brings it under control
- **Cost savings**: 5–15% savings on addressable spend through better sourcing and compliance
- **Supplier risk reduction**: Proactive visibility into supplier financial, ESG, and operational risk
- **Invoice automation**: Reduce invoice processing costs from $10–$15/invoice to under $2
- **Touchless processing**: 70–90% of transactions can be automated with catalogs and network connections
- **Contract compliance**: Prevent maverick spending and ensure negotiated prices are used at point of purchase
- **Working capital optimization**: Early payment programs and supply chain financing improve cash flow
- **ESG and supply chain transparency**: Track Scope 3 emissions, supplier diversity, conflict minerals

## Pain Points Addressed
- Significant spend happening outside of contracts (maverick buying)
- No visibility into total spend by supplier, category, or business unit
- Slow, paper-based or email-based RFP process
- Manual supplier onboarding taking weeks; high risk from unknown suppliers
- Invoice processing backlogs, high error rates, disputes with suppliers
- No early warning system for supply chain disruptions
- Procurement policy compliance only checked after the fact
- Fragmented supplier base with too many single-source relationships
- Difficulty demonstrating procurement value to the business
- Lack of supplier diversity data for ESG reporting

## Discovery Angles — What to Probe
- **Spend visibility**: What percentage of total company spend is under active management? Do you know where every dollar is going?
- **Maverick spending**: How much purchasing happens outside of approved contracts or catalogs? What's the impact?
- **Supplier risk**: How do you know if a key supplier is in financial distress or has an ESG issue? How long would it take you to find out?
- **Sourcing cycle time**: How long does it typically take from identifying a need to executing a contract? What slows it down?
- **Invoice processing**: How many invoices do you process per month? What percentage are exceptions? How many people does it take?
- **Contract compliance**: When a contract is negotiated, how confident are you that buyers are actually using those terms?
- **Supply chain disruption**: In the last 2 years, what supply chain disruptions have you experienced? How did you respond?
- **ESG goals**: Do you have supplier diversity, sustainability, or Scope 3 emissions reporting requirements?
- **ECC/S4 integration**: Are you looking to connect procurement data directly to your financial systems?

## Target Personas
- CPO / VP Procurement: Wants spend visibility, supplier risk control, savings tracking
- CFO: Wants to reduce invoice processing costs and increase working capital
- Supply Chain VP: Needs supply chain resilience and multi-tier visibility
- Sourcing Category Managers: Want better tools for sourcing events and contracts
- AP Manager: Wants to eliminate invoice exceptions and move to touchless processing
- Risk / Compliance Officer: Needs supplier risk and ESG compliance visibility
- CIO/IT: Wants to reduce number of procurement applications and move to cloud
