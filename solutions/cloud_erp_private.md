# SAP Cloud ERP — Private Cloud Edition

## Solution Overview
SAP Cloud ERP Private Cloud (S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition, sold as RISE with SAP) is SAP's managed private cloud ERP for companies with complex requirements, significant existing SAP investments, or business processes that cannot be standardised. It runs on a dedicated tenant on SAP-managed hyperscaler infrastructure (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud) and supports system conversion from SAP ECC 6.0 — allowing customers to migrate existing configuration and custom code while moving to the cloud. It is the right choice for existing SAP customers preparing for the 2027 ECC end-of-mainstream-maintenance deadline, or complex enterprises in regulated industries that need the full S/4HANA capability set with greater flexibility.

## Key Capabilities

### Everything in Public Cloud Edition, plus:
- Custom ABAP code allowed (clean core guidance provided, not enforced)
- Full S/4HANA capability set including advanced industry-specific functions
- System conversion (brownfield) path from ECC 6.0 — existing configuration migrated
- Selective data transition for companies wanting to restructure during migration
- New implementation (greenfield) also supported

### Advanced Industry Capabilities
- Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) — full warehouse automation, slotting, labour management
- Transportation Management (TM) — freight tendering, carrier collaboration, freight costing
- Plant Maintenance and Asset Management — full PM module with predictive maintenance integration
- Production Planning with detailed scheduling (PP/DS) for complex manufacturing
- Advanced Available-to-Promise (aATP) for complex fulfilment scenarios
- Environment, Health & Safety (EHS) for regulated industries
- Project Systems (PS) for project-based businesses

### RISE with SAP Bundle Inclusions
- S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition licence
- SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) — integration, extension, analytics
- SAP Signavio — process intelligence and mining for transformation insights
- SAP LeanIX — enterprise architecture management
- Cloud ALM — application lifecycle management
- Business network starter pack (SAP Business Network / Ariba)

## Business Value Propositions
- **Protect ECC investment**: Migrate existing configuration, processes, and integrations — not a rip-and-replace
- **Managed infrastructure**: SAP manages hosting, patching, backups, and performance on hyperscaler of your choice
- **Path to innovation**: Access to S/4HANA AI features, SAP BTP extensions, and new capabilities on a defined roadmap
- **ECC deadline solution**: Structured approach to moving off ECC before 2027 maintenance end
- **Reduced total cost**: Eliminate on-premise data centre costs; SAP manages the stack below the application
- **Industry depth**: Full capability set for complex industries — no compromise on functionality
- **Compliance and security**: SOC 2, ISO 27001, country-specific compliance; dedicated tenant for data isolation

## Pain Points Addressed
- SAP ECC 6.0 approaching end of mainstream maintenance (2027) with no clear migration path
- On-premise infrastructure costs escalating — data centre refresh or lease expiry
- Inability to access new SAP innovations (AI, analytics, supply chain) on aging ECC platform
- Years of custom ABAP code that can't simply be abandoned with a greenfield approach
- Complex, regulated, or industry-specific processes that can't be run on a standardised template
- IT team spending most of their budget on keeping the lights on, not delivering new capabilities
- Fragmented SAP landscape (ECC + BW + multiple add-ons) that is expensive to maintain

## Discovery Angles — What to Probe
- **ECC timeline**: Have you received SAP's end-of-mainstream-maintenance communication for 2027? What is your current plan?
- **Custom code volume**: How much custom ABAP do you have? Have you done a custom code analysis? What would be painful to give up?
- **Infrastructure situation**: Are you hosting SAP on-premise today? When does your data centre contract or hardware refresh come up?
- **Migration approach**: Have you evaluated greenfield vs. brownfield? What are the arguments internally for keeping your existing configuration?
- **SAP landscape complexity**: Beyond ECC core, what other SAP systems are you running? (BW, PI/PO, GRC, SRM, etc.)
- **Industry-specific requirements**: Are there processes in your business — warehouse automation, project accounting, transportation — that require capabilities beyond core ERP?
- **RISE awareness**: Are you familiar with the RISE with SAP bundle? Have you had a conversation with SAP about what's included?
- **Decision timeline**: Is there a board-level directive or budget cycle driving when this decision needs to be made?
