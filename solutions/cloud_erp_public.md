# SAP Cloud ERP — Public Cloud Edition

## Solution Overview
SAP Cloud ERP Public Cloud (S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, sold as GROW with SAP) is SAP's standardised, multi-tenant cloud ERP for companies that want rapid time-to-value. It delivers best-practice business processes across Finance, Procurement, Supply Chain, Sales, and Manufacturing out of the box — with quarterly innovation updates, embedded AI, and a clean-core architecture that eliminates technical debt. It is the right choice for companies moving off legacy non-SAP ERP, growing businesses, or subsidiaries that need a modern system quickly without large implementation risk.

## Key Capabilities

### Finance & Controlling
- Universal Journal — single source of truth for FI and CO in one data model
- Continuous accounting — period-end tasks distributed across the month; close in 1–2 days
- Predictive accounting — anticipate financial impact of business events before they post
- Multi-GAAP, multi-currency, multi-ledger in a single system
- Group Reporting and consolidation natively embedded
- Treasury and Cash Management with real-time liquidity forecasting
- AI-driven Accounts Payable and Receivable automation with intelligent matching

### Order-to-Cash
- Order management with available-to-promise (ATP) and credit checks
- Revenue recognition automation (IFRS 15 / ASC 606)
- Billing and invoicing automation; customer credit management

### Procure-to-Pay
- Purchase requisition and PO automation with guided buying experience
- Three-way match with exception-based processing
- Supplier invoice management with AI-based extraction
- Native integration to SAP Ariba for strategic sourcing

### Supply Chain Execution
- Inventory management and goods movements in real time
- Inbound/outbound delivery and warehouse management (basic)
- Integration to SAP IBP for demand and supply planning

### SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP)
- Extend without modifying core — side-by-side extensions on BTP
- Prebuilt integrations via Integration Suite; no custom middleware needed
- SAP Analytics Cloud embedded for dashboards and planning

## Business Value Propositions
- **Fastest time-to-value**: Go-live in 3–6 months using SAP Best Practices and preconfigured content
- **Clean core**: No custom code inside the ERP — extensions go on BTP; upgrades are seamless
- **Continuous innovation**: Quarterly releases deliver AI, automation, and new capabilities automatically
- **Real-time insight**: Live dashboards, no batch runs, drill from P&L to transaction instantly
- **Reduce manual work**: AI automation eliminates 60–80% of manual journal entries and invoice touchpoints
- **Lower TCO**: Multi-tenant cloud; no infrastructure, no patching, no upgrade projects
- **Regulatory compliance**: Built-in localisation for 50+ countries; always current for legal changes

## Pain Points Addressed
- Legacy non-SAP ERP (Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, Epicor, Infor) reaching end of life or becoming too costly
- Inability to close books in under a week without large finance team
- Disconnected systems preventing a real-time view of business performance
- Manual spreadsheet-based reporting and reconciliation consuming the finance team
- Inability to scale into new geographies or business lines without adding headcount
- IT team spending too much time maintaining and patching on-premise infrastructure
- Long, expensive ERP upgrade cycles preventing access to new capabilities

## Discovery Angles — What to Probe
- **Current ERP**: What system are you running today? What are the top frustrations your CFO and operations team have with it?
- **Financial close**: How long does month-end take? How many people are involved? What are the biggest bottlenecks?
- **Reporting**: When do you have a real view of last month's performance? How long does it take to get to the underlying data?
- **Implementation risk**: Have you had a painful ERP implementation before? What would need to be different this time?
- **Growth agenda**: Are you planning to open new geographies, acquire companies, or launch new business lines? How does your current system handle that?
- **Clean core appetite**: How much custom code does your current ERP have? What's the maintenance cost of that?
- **Timeline driver**: Is there an event creating urgency — end-of-support, merger, compliance deadline?
